Upon arrival at Agatti Airport, our representative will meet you and escort you to your hotel. Once you reach, enjoy a refreshing welcome drink, check-in, relax and head for lunch at the hotel's restaurant. In the evening, you will leave for a guided tour of the island, exploring the Agatti Museum, the Fish Hatchery, the Eastern Jetty and the Blue Lagoon Beach. Wrap up the day with a delicious dinner by the beach!

Get set to experience the beauty of Thinnakara Island! Set sail on a boat from Agatti, making your way across sparkling blue waters. Spend some time by the shore, dip your toes and unwind. As the day draws to a close, board the ferry back to Agatti and then head to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Today, you are free to spend the day at leisure! Take a pick from fun adventure activities, unwind by the beaches under swaying palms or simply relax at your hotel. Later in the evening, set out to visit the Rocky Island of Kalpitti. Famed for its intriguing local culture, you can interact with the people here, shop for handicrafts and taste authentic curries. Finally, return to your hotel in Agatti for a comfy overnight stay.

Indulge in optional adventure activities and relax by the sparkling shores of Agatti Island on this day! You can enjoy experiences like scuba diving, snorkelling, glass bottom boating and more on direct payment basis.

After breakfast, check-out from your hotel and leave for Agatti Airport for your flight back home."
